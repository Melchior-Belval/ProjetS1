Objectives



The objectives of this journey

  • Learn a foreign language

    • Nowadays, being bilingual is essential. How better could you learn a foreign language than going abroad ? To master a language, the best way is to discuss a lot with natives of the country.

    Going abroad to study could help you get a technical vocabulary for a given sector like finance, computer science or communication. This is what recruiters agencies are looking for. Apart from the professionnal aspect of learning a new language, you could meet new friends easily and some people even settle in a foreign country after this wonderful experience.

    Obviously, as for any language, the learning process can be long (2 or 3 months in general) but in the long run, it's very worth. Half a year abroad is a really good start.

  • Discover a new way of life

    • Even if many cultures were partly uniformized, each one has its inimitable side. Therefore, discovering new cultures leads to open-mindedness and a wider knowledge.
    You will know new ways of thinking, live great moments and you might see things differently. Finally, as you will sharpen your critical mind, you will become able to judge better in many situations.

  • Enjoy your stay !

    • All alone in this journey, you will learn to be responsible and you will gain maturity due to the fact that you will be living on your own. If you do great, you will gain a lot of self-confidence and self-knowledge.
    Moreover, you will become to make friends easier and speaking with people will become even more natural. You might come back as a whole new person.

  • Learn in a different way

    • Courses are not organized the same way in every country. Therefore, teaching methods are different too so you will have to adapt to these new rules in order to succeed. As an example, English speaking countries are more likely to leave students think by themselves and IT is more present in their way of working : most of their work is done on computers and sent to professors by the internet, on the digital workspace.
    Are you scared? No worries! If your student record has been accepted, it means that you are good enough to succeed. Foreign schools make their selection by analyzing your student record, so they can get an idea of what type of person you are and if it could be worth to host you for a moment.

  • Enhance your CV

    • Putting this stay as an experience on your CV can be really interesting as many recruiters are searching for people who have been to a foreign country for a job-related reason. Moreover, it means that you are at ease with other people or when you have no bearings.
    Then, it shows how independent, curious and autonomous you are. To conclude, don’t forget to add this experience in your CV because it can make you stand out from the other applicants and help you find a job.
